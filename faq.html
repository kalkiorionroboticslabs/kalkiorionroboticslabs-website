Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our programs, kits, certifications, and more.

For Schools & Institutes

Is this a robotics class or something more?

It’s not just a class. It’s a long-term learning system with kits, projects, assessments, certificates, and competitions—designed to run year after year.

Why shouldn’t we just buy kits and teach internally?

Kits provide hardware. We provide structure, progression, verification, and continuity—things schools often struggle to maintain.

Is this program affordable for average schools?

Yes. Kits are reusable, each supports 15+ projects, annual renewal is low, and costs typically range from ₹250–₹500 per student per month.

Is this suitable for CBSE / ICSE / other boards?

Yes. The program supports experiential learning and STEM skill development without claiming formal board equivalence.

What support do schools get?

Academic support, platform access, teacher enablement, documentation and resource support.

Are competitions mandatory?

No, competitions are optional but highly motivating and retention-driven.

Can this program be funded via CSR?

Yes. The program is measurable, auditable, and reusable—perfect for CSR initiatives.

How long do schools typically run this program?

Designed for continuity, schools usually run it for 3–5 years with the same kits.

What happens if a school stops after one year?

Dashboard access, new certificates, and competition eligibility stop, but previously issued certificates remain disappear.

Are there any hidden charges?

No. All costs are upfront and transparent.

What if teachers change?

Teacher certification is brand-linked, not personal. New teachers can be onboarded without restarting the program.

Do teachers need prior experience?

No. Comprehensive teacher enablement and documentation ensure anyone can guide students effectively.

For Students

Do students really build robots themselves?

Yes. Projects unlock only after submission and assessment—no copy-paste learning.

What exactly do students gain?

Real problem-solving skills, confidence in building systems, project portfolios, verified, time-stamped certificates.

How many students can one kit support?

Typically 6–10 students per batch; kits can be reused across batches.

Is this safe for younger students?

Yes. Projects are grade-appropriate and always supervised.

Is internet required?

Basic internet is required for dashboards and certificate verification.

For Parents

Can parents verify their child’s learning?

Yes. QR codes let parents view projects completed, learning hours, and certificate status—no blind trust required.

Can parents be charged directly?

Optional. The program supports school-paid or parent-paid models.

How are certificates different from others?

Certificates are QR-verified, project-backed, platform-issued, and time-stamped, showing exactly what a student built, not just attendance.

Why Choose KalkiOrion Robotics Labs?

Why should we choose you over others?

We deliver verified outcomes, not just promises—structured learning, measurable results, and tangible student growth.

Can this program scale across multiple classes or schools?

Yes. Kits, dashboards, and teacher enablement allow easy scaling across batches, grades, or even multiple institutions.
