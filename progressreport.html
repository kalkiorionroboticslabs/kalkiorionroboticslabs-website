How It Works

1. Get Your Unique ID

Every enrolled student receives a printed certificate with a unique QR code and Student ID. This ID is your key to the dashboard.

2. Scan or Enter ID

Use your phone to scan the QR code, or type your ID in the search box above. The page will instantly display your portfolio.

3. Track & Share

See your current project, past projects, and next goals. Share your profile link with parents or teachers anytime.
