Privacy Policy

KalkiOrion Robotics Labs (Origin of X Games)

Effective Date: 12 February 2026
Website: kalkiorionroboticslabs.in
Registered Office: DLF Phase 5, Golf Course Road, Sector 43, Gurugram, Haryana, India
Contact Email: info@kalkiorionroboticslabs.in

1. Introduction

KalkiOrion Robotics Labs (“KalkiOrion”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of students, parents/guardians, schools, institutions, trainers, competition participants, website visitors, and business partners.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, disclose, and safeguard personal information when you interact with our website, robotics and STEM programs, school licensing model, competitions, training services, and related offerings.

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act), 2023 (India)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – European Union)
  • UAE Federal Decree Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection
  • Other applicable international data protection regulations

2. Scope of Services

We provide structured and long-term:

  • Robotics and STEM education programs
  • School licensing models
  • Teacher and trainer workshops
  • Competitions and league events
  • Certification programs

This Policy applies to all services delivered online or offline.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our services.

A. Personal Information
Full name, Email address, Phone number, School or institution name, Grade or class details, Billing and payment information, Postal address.

B. Student Data (Collected Through Schools)
Enrollment details, Attendance records, Assessment scores, Project submissions, Competition performance data.
We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data unless required by law and supported by explicit consent.

C. Technical Information
IP address, Browser type and version, Device information, Cookies and analytics data.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal data strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering robotics and STEM programs
  • Managing school licensing and renewals
  • Conducting competitions and events
  • Processing payments and issuing certificates
  • Improving curriculum, technology, and service quality
  • Legal compliance and official communication

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual necessity (e.g., school licensing agreements)
  • Explicit consent (parents, guardians, participants)
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations

6. User Responsibilities

By accessing or using our services, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete information
  • Refrain from misusing training materials or resources
  • Not copy, reproduce, distribute, or resell curriculum or proprietary materials
  • Respect all intellectual property rights

7. Intellectual Property Rights

All curriculum content, robotics designs, competition formats, branding, digital assets, documentation, and materials are the exclusive intellectual property of KalkiOrion Robotics Labs (Origin of X Games). Unauthorized reproduction, redistribution, or commercial use is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share data only when necessary with:

  • Authorized trainers and school administrators
  • Event and competition partners (limited to event execution)
  • Payment gateway providers
  • Government authorities when legally required

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

9. Payment and Billing Practices

All fees are non-refundable unless expressly stated in writing. Annual license renewal is mandatory for continued program access. Delayed payments may result in temporary suspension of services.

10. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Student academic records: Up to 5 years after program completion
  • Financial records: As required by statutory regulations
  • Marketing and consent data: Until consent is withdrawn

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymized.

11. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure cloud storage with encryption at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Confidentiality agreements for staff
  • Regular security audits

While we take reasonable precautions, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

12. Cookies Policy

We use essential and analytics cookies to ensure website functionality, measure performance, and improve user experience. You may disable cookies via browser settings, although certain features may not function properly.

13. Children’s Privacy

Student data is collected only through school authorization or verified parental consent. We do not directly market to children under 18 years of age. Parents or guardians may request access, correction, or deletion of their child’s data at any time.

14. Media and Competition Consent

Participation in competitions, workshops, events, leagues, or showcases constitutes consent (or parental consent for minors) to:

  • Photography
  • Video recording
  • Live streaming
  • Use of media for marketing, website, social media, YouTube, brochures, and academic publications

No financial compensation is provided. Consent may be withdrawn prospectively via written notice to info@kalkiorionroboticslabs.in.

15. International Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside India, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Other lawful cross-border transfer mechanisms

16. Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request deletion (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority

To exercise your rights, contact: info@kalkiorionroboticslabs.in

17. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, our total liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the preceding six months. We are not liable for: indirect or consequential damages, internal school data mishandling, network failures, force majeure events.

18. Termination of Services

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate services in cases of: policy violations, payment default, intellectual property misuse.

19. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India. For users located in the EU or UAE, applicable local consumer protection and data protection laws supplement this Policy.

20. School Data Protection Addendum (DPA)

This Addendum forms an integral part of all School Licensing Agreements.

Roles: School: Data Controller / Data Fiduciary; KalkiOrion Robotics Labs: Data Processor

Scope: We process student data solely for robotics and STEM training, assessment and certification, competition participation.

We will not: sell or rent student data, use data for unrelated marketing, share data without explicit school authorization.

Security and Sub-Processors: All sub-processors, including cloud providers, payment gateways, and certification platforms, are bound by equivalent data protection obligations.

Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach, affected schools will be notified within 72 hours, including an impact assessment and corrective action plan.

Termination: Upon agreement termination, student data will be returned or securely deleted within 60 days, except where retention is required for statutory compliance.

21. Competition Media Waiver Clause

Registration forms may include the following consent checkbox:

“By participating in any KalkiOrion Robotics Labs competition, event, workshop, or showcase, I (or my parent/guardian if I am a minor) consent to photography, video recording, live streaming, and use of my likeness and work for marketing, website, social media, YouTube, brochures, and academic publications. No financial compensation is payable. Consent may be withdrawn prospectively by written notice.”

22. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

23. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The revised version will be posted on our website with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

24. Contact and Grievance Redressal

Grievance Officer (DPDP Act, 2023): Ankan Chakraborty

Email: info@kalkiorionroboticslabs.in
We respond to privacy-related requests within 15 business days.
