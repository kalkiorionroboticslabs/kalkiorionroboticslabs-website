Implementation Model

Three simple phases from setup to showcase.

Phase 1

Infrastructure Setup

Month 1–2: Lab installation, equipment deployment, branding with CSR Partner Logo.

Phase 2

Training & Curriculum

Month 3–12: Weekly practical sessions, project-based learning, internal assessments.

Phase 3

Innovation & Showcase

Month 12+: Robotics competition, startup idea incubation, internship connect.
