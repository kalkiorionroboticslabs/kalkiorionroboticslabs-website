Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about certifications and competitions.

How do I start the certification ladder?
Simply enroll in our Robotics Mastery course (offline/online). You'll begin at Level 1 and progress by completing projects. Each level unlocks after submitting the required project and passing a skill check.
Are the certificates recognized by schools/colleges?
Yes! Our QR-verified certificates are widely accepted by schools for co-curricular credits and by universities during admissions as proof of practical skills. Many partner schools integrate our ladder into their STEM curriculum.
Can I participate in competitions without being enrolled in courses?
Some competitions are open to all students (with a nominal registration fee), but most advanced levels require certification progression to ensure fair play and skill readiness. Check individual event pages for eligibility.
How long does it take to complete all 5 levels?
On average, dedicated students complete Level 1 in 2 months, Level 2 in 3 months, Level 3 in 3 months, Level 4 in 4 months, and Level 5 in 6 months. However, you can go at your own pace.
Do you offer certifications for schools as institutions?
Absolutely! We provide institutional partnerships where schools get access to the entire ladder, teacher training, and a dashboard to track student progress. Contact us for a customized plan.
